About Me

hi, i’m yasamean! i’m a software creative based in los angeles, united states. i’m a multi-hyphenate with a love for many things – from languages & literature to data science. i’m passionate about the intersection between humanity and technology, using data to tell stories that connect us and reveal the innate details of simple life.

flip me inside and out and i'm both logical & creative one. i’m gearing up to pursue a master’s in data science while also being a gis researcher.

when i’m not coding, you’ll find me journaling, drinking strawberry matcha, union organizing, speed puzzling, or sharing local businesses on google maps with my google maps community. ultimately, i hope to help build a space that feels welcoming, warm, and nostalgic.

My Work

I design projects that live at the intersection of code, story, and place – from data-driven civic tools to cozy, character-filled interfaces. My portfolio includes:

  • Civic & data projects like FairMap and PlotGrid that use data science, GIS, and visualization to make complex systems feel human and understandable.
  • Playful web experiences inspired by pins, music, books, and national parks – small worlds you can click into and explore.
  • Writing- and organizing-adjacent tools that support union work, storytelling, and community-building.
